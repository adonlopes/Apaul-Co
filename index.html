A Paul & Co is a multidisciplinary firm specializing in architecture, planning, liaisoning, and interior design.

Established in 2025, the firm builds upon a family legacy in architecture and construction dating back to 1991, bringing decades of experience and industry understanding to every project.

Our work combines creative architectural thinking with strategic planning and regulatory expertise, ensuring projects move seamlessly from concept to completion.

At A Paul & Co, we believe that every project begins with a clear vision, and through thoughtful design and professional coordination, that vision becomes a well-executed built environment.

Our Heritage

A Legacy Built on Decades of Excellence

1991

Company Background

Nilopher Engineering Works (N.E.W.)

The origins of the firm trace back to 1991, when Mr. Paul Lopes established Nilopher Engineering Works (N.E.W.), a specialized engineering enterprise focusing on steel structures and ancillary engineering works.

Over the next three decades, the firm built a strong reputation for technical precision, reliability, and quality execution across the Vasai–Virar and Palghar districts. Under his leadership, N.E.W. delivered numerous structural and engineering solutions, contributing to regional industrial and infrastructural development.

Through consistent dedication to craftsmanship, professionalism, and client trust, the company laid the foundation for a respected family legacy in engineering and construction.

Steel Structures Engineering Works Vasai–Virar & Palghar
34 Years of Growth
2025

New Vision

A Paul & Co

Continuing this legacy, Ar. Aaron Paul Lopes, a Bachelor of Architecture graduate from the University of Mumbai and a registered architect with the Council of Architecture, founded A Paul & Co. in 2025.

As part of this new phase, Nilopher Engineering Works (N.E.W.) was incorporated into A Paul & Co., bringing together decades of engineering expertise with contemporary architectural practice.

Today, the integrated firm offers architecture, planning, engineering consultancy, and liaison services, representing a seamless evolution from a strong engineering foundation to a forward-looking multidisciplinary practice.

Our Founder

Ar. Aaron Paul Lopes

Founder & Principal Architect

Ar. Aaron Paul Lopes is the Founder and Principal Architect of A Paul & Co. He graduated with a Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) from the University of Mumbai and is registered with the Council of Architecture (COA), India.

Coming from a family background in architecture and construction since 1991, he carries forward a legacy of professional expertise in the field of design and development.

Through A Paul & Co, he leads the firm with a vision to deliver innovative architectural solutions, strategic planning, and efficient project coordination, ensuring that every project reflects both creativity and practicality.
